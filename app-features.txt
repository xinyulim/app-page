This App is for overseas travelers who wish to avoid paying expensive roaming charges, and still remain reachable on their regular mobile numbers from their home country.
 
BENEFITS
● FREE Calls: App to App - Flexiroam App user who are online for free.
● Make calls to any landline or mobile for as low as $1 cent / minute.
● Your overseas contacts can call you through the app for free, or call directly from any mobile to any of your international numbers anytime.
● Eliminate the need for IDD and roaming call charges.
● Maintain a presence overseas with a local number in more than 55 international destinations.  


FEATURES
High quality low bandwidth calls / video
Encrypted and secure calls
Messaging - group messaging to collaborate / pictures/ files / voice messages / share location 
Wide coverage of international numbers 
Support multiple operating systems, platforms and devices
